  1. 区域截图,
  2. 截图修改,

  1. 图床分享, 默认为 本人的图床

  1. >= python2.6
  2. python-xlib
  3. python-requests
  4. xsel

#!/bin/bash
time=`date +%H:%M:%S`
msg=`scrot -s "$time.png" -e "curl -sF 'name=@$time.png' http://eleveni386.7axu.com/Image/"`
gxmessage -center -title "eleveni386.7axu.com" $msg
rm -rf $time.png
